Our Mission

At Fula Devi Memorial Sewa Sansthan, our mission is to uplift the underprivileged communities of Bihar by providing essential services and creating sustainable solutions. We are focused on:

  • Cloth Donation: We provide clothing to those in need, ensuring that everyone has access to basic necessities.
  • Food Donation: Occasionally, we offer food assistance to help those facing food insecurity.

Our Impact
