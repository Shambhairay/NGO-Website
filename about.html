About Us

Fula Devi Memorial Sewa Sansthan is a registered non-profit organization based in Patna, Bihar. Founded in May 2022, our organization is dedicated to supporting the underprivileged communities of Bihar by providing essential services, education, and assistance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift the lives of the less fortunate by addressing their most pressing needs. We aim to foster an environment where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and succeed, regardless of their economic status.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to basic human rights, quality education, and the support they need to build a better future. Our goal is to create a sustainable impact through our initiatives and partnerships, driving positive change in our communities.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We are driven by empathy and a commitment to helping those in need.
  • Excellence: We are dedicated to achieving high standards in our programs and services.

Our Activities

Our primary focus is on the following activities:

  • Cloth Donation: We provide clothing to those in need every year, ensuring that everyone has access to basic necessities.
  • Food Assistance: We offer food to those facing food insecurity, providing essential nourishment to those who need it most.
  • Educational Support: We guide underprivileged children on the right path for their education, helping them achieve success. Additionally, we provide support to those in need with their educational pursuits.

Our History

Founded in memory of Fula Devi, our organization was established with the aim of continuing her legacy of kindness and philanthropy. From its inception, Fula Devi Memorial Sewa Sansthan has been focused on providing support to those who need it the most in Bihar. Through various programs and initiatives, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of our beneficiaries.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our services and reach even more communities. Our goals for the coming years include:

  • Expanding Educational Programs: Introducing new educational programs to reach more children and youth.
  • Enhanced Support Services: Increasing our support for educational pursuits and personal development.
  • Strengthening Community Engagement: Increasing community involvement and volunteer opportunities to build a stronger support network.
